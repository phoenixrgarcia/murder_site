INVESTIGATION RULES

How to Play

Overview

A mystery has struck aboard the research vessel. One of your crew has been murdered. As players, you must work together to uncover the truth, investigate clues, and ultimately solve who committed the crime.

Getting Started

  • Each player selects a character to assume for the duration of the party
  • Read your character briefing before arriving or upon arrival
  • Each character has secrets, motives, and alibis to share

Investigation Phase

  • Players have 60 minutes to investigate clues and interview each other
  • Clues are scattered throughout the location - find them all
  • Each character may or may not be telling the truth about their whereabouts
  • Pay attention to timelines, motives, and evidence

Accusation & Resolution

  • When ready, players vote on who they believe is guilty
  • The group must reach a consensus or vote majority rules
  • The guilty party will be revealed, along with their motive and confession

Golden Rules

  • All character secrets must stay secret until the reveal
  • Have fun and commit to your role
  • Treat this as a collaborative storytelling experience
  • No one gets left out - everyone is important to the mystery

Still Have Questions?

Not sure about something? Review the character profiles for more context, or speak with the Game Master for clarifications.
